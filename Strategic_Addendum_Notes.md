## 2. Sharpened claims and numbers

- **9× ToFu lift** required to reach 25 trials/day — derived live on slide 10 instead of a buried sentence.
- **₹1 Lakh MRR** by Month 3 used as a single, stake-in-the-ground target on slides 1, 3 and 19.
- **5×** revenue upside with **zero new traffic** stated on the cover as the deck's headline argument.

---

## 3. Voice and structure changes

- Each section opens with a **diagnosis line in italics** ("88% of every leak sits in steps 2 and 3" etc.), giving the reader the *insight* before the data.
- Every recommendation slide ends with an **expected impact strip** so the reader always knows what success looks like.
- The closing slide ("The Big Bet") commits to **one strategic principle**: fix the funnel before scaling it; deepen monetization before buying traffic.
- Layout uses a **deep-indigo + saffron + cream** palette: consultant-grade, but visually consonant with a spiritual brand.
