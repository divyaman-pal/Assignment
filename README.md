## Deck thesis

Bhagavad Gita Daily does **not** have a product or audience problem.
It has a **funnel problem** and a **monetization-depth problem** — both fixable in 90 days.

Three strategic levers:

1. **Seal the funnel** — stop the bleed at homepage, trial activation and Day-3 churn.
2. **Scale top-of-funnel** — DM automation → content engine → paid acquisition (in that order).
3. **Deepen monetization** — annual plan, situation PDF guides, premium community, card deck, B2B "Corporate Gita Daily".

Target: **₹1 Lakh blended MRR by Month 3**, achievable if the funnel work lands in the first 30 days.
